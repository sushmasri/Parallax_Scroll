Natural Homemade LipBalm

Supplies Needed

  • Shea Butter-1/2 tbsp
  • Beeswax Palletes-1 tbsp
  • Coconut Oil-1 tbsp
  • Essential Oils-Peppermint/Lavender
  • Mica of preffered color
  • LipBalm Tubes

    • Double Boil shea butter, Beeswax, coconut oil

    Add essential oil,mica in melted oils

    Pour it in LipBalm container and let it solidify
